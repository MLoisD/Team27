Product Info

"Nutuk Söylev" is a book containing the full text of the famous speech given by Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, the founder of the Turkish Republic, in 1927. The speech was delivered over several days and covers the history of the Turkish War of Independence, the founding of the Republic, and the political, social, and economic reforms that were implemented during the early years of the Republic.
